Our Story

The Warehouse

Dog Warehouse is a safe haven for dogs. Dogs are the king and queen of this place. You can adopt a rescued dog!

How We Started

After graduating, me and my friends decided to have a dog shop since we love dogs very much. At first we were just a small shop but then we grew after we help rescuing dogs who are abused and let other people adopt them if they are worth it.

Our Services

The warehouse offers you different deals.

We Rescue

Our team are rescuing dogs 24/7 from all over the world and make sure that the people who abused them will be punished.

We Care

After rescuing them, the team will aid to their needs and make sure that they will feel the love that they deserved.

We Deliver

After making sure that we tend to their needs, we deliver the dogs to those who wants to adopt them.
